# Pipeline Corrosion

## Introduction

Pipeline corrosion is the gradual deterioration of pipeline materials due to chemical or electrochemical reactions with the environment.

---

## Causes

- Water
- Oxygen
- Carbon dioxide
- Hydrogen sulfide
- Poor coatings

---

## Effects

- Pipeline leaks
- Reduced equipment lifespan
- Environmental pollution
- Increased maintenance costs

---

## Prevention Methods

- Protective coatings
- Cathodic protection
- Corrosion inhibitors
- Regular inspections
- Pigging operations

---

## Conclusion

Proper corrosion management improves pipeline reliability, reduces operating costs, and protects the environment.
