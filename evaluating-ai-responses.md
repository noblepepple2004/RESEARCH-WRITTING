# Evaluating AI Responses

## Objective

AI evaluation involves assessing whether an AI-generated response is accurate, helpful, safe, and relevant.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

- Accuracy
- Clarity
- Completeness
- Logical reasoning
- Safety
- Grammar
- Relevance

---

## Example

Question:

> Explain renewable energy.

A strong response should:

- Define renewable energy
- Give examples
- Explain advantages
- Mention limitations
- Use simple language

---

## Why AI Evaluation Matters

High-quality evaluation improves AI systems by identifying incorrect, incomplete, or misleading responses.
